Background information on Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province, and Chinese villages and their traditions:
1. Hangzhou is home to the renowned dragon well (longjing) tea, ranked first among China's top 10 teas.
2. The picturesque village of Longjing sits amid some of China's most beautiful countryside and is easily accessible from Hangzhou
3. Zhejiang Province government has announced that four old Hangzhou villages would be provided up to 7 million yuan (US$990,000) in financial support this year to preserve their distinctive architecture and local ways of life.
