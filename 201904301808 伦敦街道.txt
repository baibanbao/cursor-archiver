Have you seen the old man

Kicking up the paper,
with his worn out shoes?
In his eyes you see no pride
And held loosely at his side

也看不到人类的高尚
颤巍巍他的手里捏着一张
昨天的报纸登着昨天的文章

So how can you tell me you're lonely,
