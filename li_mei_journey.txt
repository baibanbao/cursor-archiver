Rough Outline of Li Mei's Journey

1. Li Mei's upbringing in her small village in Hangzhou, China.
2. Li Mei dreams of becoming a top fashion designer in the city.
3. Li Mei's struggles and successes in becoming a top fashion designer in the city.
4. Li Mei's relationship with her strict father, Li Jilai.
5. Li Mei's close relationship with her best friends, Wu Yue and Chen Ying.
6. Li Mei's romantic relationship with Zhang Wei.
7. Li Mei's mentor, Professor Liu.
8. Li Mei's rivalry with Huang Shan, who operates a traditional qipao store.
9. The villain, Liu Taibao, a corrupted official who tries to force Li Mei to be his mistress and later tries to take revenge on her.
