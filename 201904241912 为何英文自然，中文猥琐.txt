邵风华:美国诗人理查德.布劳提根

《美好的诗》

我睡在洛杉矶想着
你

几分钟前撒尿时
我低头注视着小弟弟
深情地

知道它在你里面已经两次
今天让我
感觉美好
龚民:The Beautiful Poem
Richard Brautigan

I go to bed in Los Angeles thinking
about you.

Pissing a few moments ago
I looked down at my penis
affectionately.

Knowing it has been inside
you twice today makes me
feel beautiful.
