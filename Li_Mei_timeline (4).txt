- Li Mei was born and raised in a small village in Hangzhou, China.
- Her dream is to become the top fashion designer in the city.
- Li Mei's father, Li Jilai, is a strict man.
- Li Mei's best friends are Wu Yue, an lesbian artist, and Chen Ying, a beautiful house wife.
- Her lover is Zhang Wei.
- Professor Liu is her mentor.
- Huang Shan, who operates a traditional qipao store, is her rival.
- Liu Taibao, a corrupt official who is greedy and lustful, wants to force Li Mei into becoming his mistress. However, when she refuses, he tries to seek revenge on her.
