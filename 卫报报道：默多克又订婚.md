# 卫报报道：默多克又订婚

# **Rupert Murdoch, 92, to wed retired molecular biologist Elena Zhukova, 67\
鲁珀特·默多克，92岁，将与退休的分子生物学家埃琳娜·朱科娃，67岁结婚**

**It will be the billionaire’s fifth wedding, five months after he stepped down from his News Corp media empire\
这将是这位亿万富翁的第五次婚礼，距离他辞去新闻集团媒体帝国职位已有五个月**

***[Maya Yang 玛雅杨](https://www.theguardian.com/profile/maya-yang)***

Thu 7 Mar 2024 23.53 GMT\
2024年3月7日星期四 23:53格林尼治标准时间

**Share**

The media mogul [Rupert Murdoch](https://www.theguardian.com/media/rupert-murdoch) is engaged for a sixth time, it emerged on Thursday, this time to Elena Zhukova, a retired scientist.\
传媒大亨鲁珀特·默多克周四宣布第六次订婚，这次对象是退休科学家埃琳娜·朱科娃。

[Rupert Murdoch met Rishi Sunak five times in 12-month period\
鲁珀特·默多克在12个月的时间里与里希·苏纳克会面了五次](https://www.theguardian.com/media/2024/feb/11/rupert-murdoch-met-rishi-sunak-five-times-in-12-month-period)

**[Read more 阅读更多](https://www.theguardian.com/media/2024/feb/11/rupert-murdoch-met-rishi-sunak-five-times-in-12-month-period)**

Murdoch’s office announced that the 92-year-old is set to marry Zhukova, a 67-year-old retired molecular biologist, [according to](https://www.nytimes.com/2024/03/07/business/media/rupert-murdoch-engaged-elena-zhukova.html) the New York Times.\
据《纽约时报》报道，默多克的办公室宣布，这位92岁的默多克将与67岁的退休分子生物学家朱科娃结婚。

Last April, news reports [emerged](https://www.theguardian.com/media/2023/aug/16/rupert-murdoch-dating-again-four-months-after-breaking-off-engagement) of Murdoch dating Zhukova just four months after he ended his two-week-long engagement to Ann Lesley Smith, a 67-year old conservative radio host.\
去年四月，有新闻报道称，默多克在结束与67岁的保守派广播主持人安·莱斯利·史密斯为期两周的订婚关系后，仅仅四个月就与朱科娃约会。

Invitations for Murdoch’s wedding, which will be held at Moraga, his California vineyard and estate, have already been sent out, the Times reports, citing a representative for Murdoch, who has been married four times before.\
据《泰晤士报》报道，默多克的婚礼邀请已经发出，婚礼将在他位于加利福尼亚的莫拉加葡萄园和庄园举行。默多克此前已经结过四次婚。

Murdoch [met](https://www.theguardian.com/media/2023/aug/16/rupert-murdoch-dating-again-four-months-after-breaking-off-engagement) Zhukova through a large family gathering hosted by his third ex-wife, Wendi Deng, whom he stayed married to for 14 years before [filing ](https://www.theguardian.com/media/2013/jun/13/rupert-murdoch-divorce-wendi-deng)for divorce in 2013.\
默多克通过他的第三任前妻邓文迪主持的一次大型家庭聚会与朱科娃相识，他与邓文迪结婚14年后于2013年提出离婚。

Her 42-year-old daughter, Dasha Zhukova, is a Russian-American art collector and philanthropist who was previously married to [Roman Abramovich](https://www.theguardian.com/world/roman-abramovich), a Russian oligarch and former owner of the Premier League football club Chelsea.\
她42岁的女儿达莎·朱科娃是一位俄罗斯裔美国艺术收藏家和慈善家，曾与俄罗斯寡头、前英超足球俱乐部切尔西的所有者罗曼·阿布拉莫维奇结过婚。

News of Murdoch’s latest engagement comes just five months after he announced that he was stepping down from his media empire, which he [built](https://www.theguardian.com/media/live/2023/sep/21/rupert-murdoch-fox-news-steps-down-chairman-lachlan-latest) after his father handed him his first newspaper.\
默多克最新订婚的消息传出仅仅五个月之后，他宣布从自己的媒体帝国退下来，这个帝国是他在父亲将第一份报纸交给他之后建立起来的。

Murdoch’s publicly traded and New York-based company News Corp owns hundreds of local, national and international digital news outlets including the Wall Street Journal, Fox News and Sky News Australia, as well as the book publisher HarperCollins.\
默多克的上市公司News Corp总部位于纽约，拥有数百家本地、国家和国际数字新闻媒体，包括《华尔街日报》、福克斯新闻和澳大利亚天空新闻，以及图书出版商哈珀柯林斯。

The media mogul boasts a net worth of $8.96bn, [according to](https://www.bloomberg.com/billionaires/profiles/keith-r-murdoch/?sref=fqqmZ8gi) Bloomberg.\
根据彭博社的数据，这位媒体大亨的净资产达到89.6亿美元。

Murdoch’s new fiancee previously worked as a molecular diabetes specialist at the University of California, Los Angeles, the New York Times [reports](https://www.nytimes.com/2024/03/07/business/media/rupert-murdoch-engaged-elena-zhukova.html). Zhukova, who originally hails from Russia and once [described](https://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2010/09/27/garage-mechanics) her family as being part of the “usual, normal Moscow intelligentsia”, was married to Alexander Zhukov, a Russian-born British billionaire energy investor.\
默多克的新未婚妻此前在加利福尼亚大学洛杉矶分校担任分子糖尿病专家，据《纽约时报》报道。朱科娃原籍俄罗斯，曾形容她的家庭是“普通的、正常的莫斯科知识分子”的一部分。她曾与俄罗斯出生的英国亿万富翁能源投资者亚历山大·朱科夫结婚。

Murdoch’s last wife was the former supermodel Jerry Hall, whom he divorced in 2022. His other previous wives were Wendi Deng, Anna Murdoch Mann and Patricia Booker. He has six children.\
默多克的最后一任妻子是前超模杰瑞·霍尔，他们在2022年离婚。他的其他前妻分别是邓文迪、安娜·默多克·曼和帕特里夏·布克。他有六个孩子。

The wedding this summer will come after a year of setbacks for the Murdoch empire in 2023, including a [$787.5m](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2023/apr/18/fox-dominion-settle-us-defamation-lawsuit) settlement between Fox News and the voting equipment company Dominion. The settlement ended a dispute over whether the network and its parent company knowingly broadcast false and outlandish allegations that Dominion was involved in a plot to steal the 2020 US election.\
今年夏天的婚礼将是2023年默多克帝国一年来的挫折之后，其中包括福克斯新闻与选举设备公司Dominion之间的7.875亿美元和解。该和解结束了一场争议，争议是关于该电视网络及其母公司是否故意播放虚假和荒谬的指控，声称Dominion参与了窃取2020年美国大选的阴谋。
